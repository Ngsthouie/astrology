1. Characteristics of Taurus

Strengths: Reliable, patient, practical, dedicated, responsible, stable

Weaknesses: Stubborn, possessive, unforgiving

Taurus likes: Gardening, cooking, music, romance, high-quality clothes, working with their hands

Taurus dislikes: Sudden changes, complications, unsafety of any kind, synthetic fabrics

Taurus people are often said to be hot on the inside and cold on the outside, and they are often considered moody people. They usually don't like to be angry, they don't want to embarrass others. Taurus people are often very funny, and they don't react as slowly as others say, they're just too lazy to show it. When they are really angry, they will never tell anyone the reason, and they also never say a word, which often makes people feel inexplicable.

Venus rules Taurus, so Taurus is a conservative constellation, does not like change, stability is his attitude to life. People of the Taurus zodiac sign are not hot-tempered and impulsive but are patient. Unemployment is the problem Taurus fears most because lack of security means their lives lose focus.

They don't want to be wrong, and they hate being wrong. They are naturally melancholy and depressed, and are extremely patient. When these substances accumulate to the peak, they will explode like a volcano. In front of everyone, they appear naive and a bit cheeky, but in reality they are very smart and careful, in their eyes they are actually understanding people. They hate explaining or arguing about something because they don't think it makes any sense and they just don't understand it. Therefore, people always mistakenly think that Taurus knows nothing.
