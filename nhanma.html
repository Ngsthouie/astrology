1. Characteristics of Sagittarius

Strengths: Generosity, idealism, sense of humor

Weaknesses: Promises more than can be delivered, very impatient, will say anything no matter how unusual

Sagittarius likes: Freedom, travel, philosophy.

Sagittarius doesn't like: People who are entangled, constrained, theoretical, and detailed.

Sagittarius enjoys all outdoor activities with lots of space. People of this zodiac sign tend to explore unknown areas, both spiritually and practically, believing that life is the sum of a series of challenges. He is full of curiosity about everything.

For Sagittarius people, ideals and vision in life are often more important than goals. When young, Sagittarius is very reckless, loves the thrill of driving but rarely cares about safety, tends to pursue excessive stimulation, by nature he is very optimistic, even a bit optimistic. blindly observe. Therefore, Sagittarius learns from mistakes more often than other zodiac signs, but when they get old, it is easy to forget the recklessness of youth and become old-fashioned. Although he has never changed his habits of personal freedom, he has always been able to develop his intelligence and innate potential, potentially involving fields related to philosophy.

Sagittarius is also very good at thinking, loves to pursue truth and justice, and likes to explore areas that they do not understand well. Language is a tool for Sagittarius to expand knowledge, so Sagittarius actively expands language abilities in many aspects. They often focus on things that are beyond their ability to pursue such as simple and accessible goals, and are always eager to engage in new plans before completing one.

Like Gemini, Sagittarius people are naturally versatile and often engage in many types of work; They have strong ambitions and are very good at governing, very suitable for a political career; they often do mental work, so they need a lot of exercise to relieve stress. When they feel exhausted, it's mostly due to monotony, and simply changing jobs can restore their usual anger.
