INTRODUCTION TO ASTROLOGICAL CHARTS

In astrology, human beings are influenced not only by genetic and environmental factors but also by the energetic states of the solar system at the time and place of their birth. Planets are considered the fundamental energies of life, and these planets form various aspects, connections, and relationships with each other based on their positions in the zodiac signs.

By understanding the roles of these planets and their specific attributes (elements, houses, zodiac signs), astrology helps us gain a comprehensive and detailed picture of a person's potential. When using this astrology chart service, you won't encounter difficulties in reading the automatically generated reports based on your provided birthdate, time, and place. You don't need to know any scientific terminology to explore your own chart.

Here, to use the website's service, you need to know your date of birth, especially your time of birth and place of birth. With this information, we will create an astrological chart or birth chart. This chart will include the positions of celestial bodies like the Sun, Moon, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, Pluto, as well as the Ascendant and Midheaven points. The significance of each planet, house, and other elements will be explained in detail in your chart.

DIFFERENT TYPES OF ASTROLOGICAL CHARTS

Explore various types of astrological charts with HIPPO, including personal birth charts, couple's charts, and methods for future predictions.

Personal Birth Chart

A personal birth chart (also known as a natal chart) is based on an individual's time of birth and represents a map of the celestial sky at the moment of their birth. It reveals the positions of the planets in space at that specific time and provides a detailed insight into personality, strengths, challenges, and life path. It's an essential tool for self-discovery and understanding one's destiny.

Couple's Synastry Chart

A couple's synastry chart is a tool used to assess the harmony and potential challenges between two individuals in a relationship. By comparing the positions of planets and points in the birth charts of both individuals, it offers a detailed view of the dynamics and compatibility of the couple. This tool helps understand strengths and areas that need development in the relationship, guiding couples toward a deeper connection and a better understanding of each other in their relationship.

Future Prediction Chart

Transits represent the relationship between current entities in the solar system and the planets in a birth chart, showing progress, movement, and the impact of current planets on your personal development over time. By understanding these movements, you gain insights into life events and personal growth, supporting decision-making and self-awareness.
