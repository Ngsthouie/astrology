1. Characteristics of Capricorn

Strengths: Responsible, disciplined, self-controlled, good management

Weaknesses: Know-it-all, unforgiving, condescending, expecting the worst

Capricorn likes: Family, tradition, music, low status, quality craftsmanship

Capricorn dislikes: Almost everything at some point

Capricorn is the zodiac sign that symbolizes the beginning of winter. Winter gives absolute consciousness to those born under the sign of Capricorn. Capricorn people, especially when there are many planets at once, you will be realistic and ambitious; At the same time, you are easily conquered by passionate feelings and you have a strong altruistic spirit. Your expression is calm and indifferent, unapproachable and likes to be alone.

Capricorns are career-oriented and often put their personal lives aside. Everything starts from the most practical point of view, starting from scratch and pursuing tangible results. You crave success, perhaps to compensate for some inner need or a deep sense of loneliness, but you never confuse your relationships with your career. Your logical thinking, objective attitude and organizational mindset can put you in high-ranking positions, and sometimes lead you down the path of social or political life. Many national leaders are governed by the influence of Capricorn in your horoscope.

Generally speaking, in youth, due to lack of enthusiasm, it is not easy to win the favor of others, always having difficulties, sometimes even difficult. However, this situation will stimulate your determination to fight for a bright future. If you can make your career, your intellectual superiority will become more and more obvious as you grow up. . From the age of fifty onwards, Capricorn people can enter the golden age of your life. In old age, you will reach a state of spiritual and material perfection, and you will taste the sweet fruits of your labor.

Capricorns cherish the wealth earned through their hard work. Spending is very planned, does not like to waste. Saving is its true essence. You are more likely to excel in traditional, methodical work than in innovative work. Especially when Saturn is in Capricorn or Aquarius.
