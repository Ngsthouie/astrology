1. Characteristics of Leo

Strengths: Creative, passionate, generous, warm, cheerful, humorous

Weaknesses: Arrogant, stubborn, self-centered, lazy, inflexible

Leo likes: Stage, holidays, being admired, expensive things, bright colors, fun with friends

Leo dislikes: Being ignored, facing difficult realities, not being treated like a king or queen

Leo is ruled by the sun god Apollo, so enthusiasm, confidence and generosity are all his characteristics. Furthermore, his natural leadership ability makes him enjoy commanding others and has strong organizational skills, but overconfidence becomes arrogant, coupled with a stubborn personality, exaggerated reactions and sometimes people don't know how to get along with him. Leos, who like to be the center of attention, are very vulnerable; because they care too much about what others think of them, they are often unhappy; Their unwillingness to admit failure is also a source of unhappiness. Leo women are even more pitiful, they are the constellation that is afraid of old age, loneliness and not being respected by others. To avoid things happening to them, they will do everything possible to change them.

Leo's traits are clear at a glance, with no hidden complications or confusion. King, boss, everything, he is the leader of the group, and he is well aware of his ability to control and lead others. This zodiac sign is not only good at leadership, but can also lead by example and work hard. This trend is even more obvious when the Sun is in Leo. This constellation has the disadvantages of being arrogant, arbitrary, and intolerant of others, so it is necessary to restrain yourself and self-reflect to make the best use of inherent advantages such as helpfulness, generosity.
