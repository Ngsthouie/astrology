1. Characteristics of Pisces

Strengths: Compassionate, artistic, intuitive, gentle, wise, musical

Weaknesses: Fear, overconfidence, sadness, wanting to escape reality, can be a victim or martyr

Pisces likes: Being alone, love, sleeping, music, romance, swimming, spiritual topics

Pisces dislikes: Knowing it all, being criticized, the past coming back to haunt, cruelty of any kind

Pisces is the last sign of the zodiac, perhaps because of that, they are always stuck between spirit and desire, retreating into a dreamland of their own creation. They love to dream, are always imagining and they often transfer this complexity into the real environment, which makes it seem unrealistic, but they are kind and have a spirit of sacrifice and dedication to help others; They are sensitive, kind, generous, indifferent, gentle, have pure feelings, and are also the most "amorous" of the 12 constellations.

Pisces is a strange and complex constellation, containing too many levels of emotions, so there are great ups and downs, contradictions, sharp sensitivity, erudite intelligence, poetic writing, and delicate emotions. every upheaval has produced unparalleled artistic genius.

All in all, this is an image full of divinity, magic, understanding, strong observation, lack of confidence, (if female, weak self-control), and changeability. Mysterious constellation.

The zodiac symbol of Pisces is exactly two fish swimming in opposite directions, one up and the other down; There's nothing better to describe the complex personality of Pisces.
