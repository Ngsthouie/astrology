1. Characteristics of the Cancer

Strengths: Skillful, imaginative, loyal, emotional, sympathetic, persuasive

Weaknesses: Sad, pessimistic, suspicious, manipulative, insecure

Cancer likes: Art, hobbies at home, relaxing near or in the water, helping loved ones, a good meal with friends

Cancer dislikes: Strangers, any criticism of mother, revelations of personal life

When talking about Cancer, it is impossible not to mention their motherly nature of loving family, but don't forget that being full of love is a Cancer characteristic. Just like their marked crab counterparts, they have a hard shell but soft heart, so Cancer people know how to protect themselves. Cancer is a water sign so it cannot avoid persistent emotions and memories. They will also ponder over trivial things but are very thoughtful and kind to the people they love.

Their ruling planet is the Moon, which affects mood and the digestive system. When you first get to know Cancer, you will be deceived by your first impression of him, because they need to protect himself a lot and won't open up to strangers, but when you get to know him better, you'll know that he is. really a good person. Among the 12 zodiac signs, Cancer is the most steadfast, loyal, persistent with friends and lovers, values ​​family, likes to collect and keep things the most, never gives up anything. anything, and They also have quite a taste for flashy things.

Cancer is sentimental by nature, prone to melancholy and daydreaming, he often reminisces about the good old days and tends to live in the darkness of the past (Mercury in Cancer strengthens this personality ). But he can also completely grasp the present. Cancer people have an indomitable will. Once they have made a plan, they must carry it out.

Cancer people are tolerant people, often not brooding over trivial issues. They are tolerant of others, rarely reject people thousands of kilometers away, along with a polite, sociable and humorous personality. And respect humanism, there will be many friends. However, sometimes for personal gain we are too liberal, we need to avoid unnecessary extravagance.
