WHAT IS NUMEROLOGY?

Numerology is a discipline that delves deep into the vibrational interplay between numbers and individuals. According to numerology, each number carries a unique meaning and energy, and they are applied to analyze aspects of personality, abilities, life missions, and various facets of an individual's life.

APPLICATION OF NUMEROLOGY IN LIFE





1. Self-Analysis

Numerology will help you gain a better understanding of yourself, including your personality, abilities, strengths, weaknesses, and more. With this knowledge, you can leverage your strengths and address your weaknesses to lead a happier and more successful life.

2. Naming Your Child

Naming your child based on numerology is a way for parents to choose a name that aligns with their child's personality, abilities, and preferences.

3. Career Guidance

Exploring numerology can provide insights into your interests, abilities, and strengths. This understanding can help you choose a career path that suits you and enables you to achieve success in your profession.

4. Love Compatibility

Consulting numerology can help you gain a better understanding of the personality and preferences of your partner. With this insight, you can seek a compatible life partner for a lasting and happy relationship.

INSTRUCTIONS ON HOW TO CALCULATE NUMEROLOGY

To calculate your numerology number, you need to add all the digits in your Gregorian date of birth (day, month, and year) together until you have a single-digit result ranging from 1 to 9 or three special cases, 11, 22, or 33.

For example:

  • If your date of birth is: 26/09/1996
  • You need to add all the digits together: 2 + 6 + 0 + 9 + 1 + 9 + 9 + 6 = 42
  • Then, continue to add the two digits 4 and 2 together: 4 + 2 = 6
  • So, your core numerology number is 6.
