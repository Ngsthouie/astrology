1. Characteristics of Aries

Strengths: Courageous, steadfast, confident, enthusiastic, optimistic, honest, passionate

Weaknesses: Impatient, erratic, hot-tempered, impulsive, aggressive

Aries likes: Comfortable clothing, taking on leadership roles, physical challenges, individual sports

Aries dislikes: Inactivity, procrastination, work that doesn't use their talents

The first sign of the zodiac is Aries, so this sign is related to beginnings. Aries is a constellation that can be seen aloft near the south at sunset in December. Two of the brightest stars are the horns of Aries. The period from March 21 to April 20, when the sun passes through this constellation, is the "peak equinox" when the nights become shorter than the days. Because spring is sunny longer, all kinds of flowers begin to bloom. Aries born around this time are full of life and energy.

Aries gives the impression of excellence and ability to work, with a deep and sharp face, with high forehead and cheekbones, strong chin and tightly pursed lips. Thick eyebrows, sharp and direct eyes, long nose. Changeable and irritable personality; he is a born warrior with powerful skills; he will show childishness in front of the people he cares about. Overall, a very cute guy!

People of the Aries zodiac sign are impulsive, adventurous, generous and fearless. Once they make up their minds, they will not die even on the Yellow River. They must overcome all difficulties to achieve their goals. pepper. People born under the sign of Aries are devoted to new things, taking risks and pursuing speed. Most Aries people have a bad temper, but they will never mind, and they will soon be fine, and the vengeful Scorpio is the opposite of Aries. Aries is the first house of the zodiac, so it likes to be the first strong constellation. In addition, Mars rules Aries, they must ignite the fire of rage, otherwise life will be bleak.
