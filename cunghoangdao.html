Basic Knowledge About Astrology - Detailed Breakdown of the 12 Zodiac Signs

In Western astrology, the 12 zodiac signs symbolize the orientation of the universe. When a person is born, the positions of the stars along the zodiac path indicate their innate personality and talents. The 12 zodiac signs represent different psychological characteristics and reflect how a person behaves. These signs are Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius, and Pisces.

The term "zodiac" refers to the "zodiac of the sun." In other words, from the perspective of Earth as the center, we observe the positions of constellations along the sun's path, and we can determine which zodiac sign a person belongs to based on where the sun was at the time of their birth.
